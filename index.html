Our Story

Our story begins in Kerala, where coffee was part of everyday life — not as a statement, but as a quiet ritual. It lived in the mornings, in family kitchens, in long conversations, and in the small pauses that make a day feel complete. Growing up around that rhythm shaped the way we think about coffee: honest, grounding, and deeply personal.

When we started building this brand, we wanted to carry that feeling forward in a way that feels modern and refined. We come from different paths, but share the same belief: good coffee should be thoughtful in taste, simple in expression, and memorable in the way it makes people feel. This brand is our way of bringing family-rooted warmth into a cleaner, more contemporary experience — one that feels at home in today’s world without losing where it came from.
